Thai Curry

An easy and delicous curry recipe

This is my go-to thai curry recipe. It is simple to make and uses only a few ingredients. Using a curry paste takes out a lot of work, and most of the ingredients are hard to find at your local grocery store!

This can easily be made vegan by adding your favoire veggie protein (mine happens to be tofu). For this reicipe we will be using chicken thigh meat as the protein. I hope you enjoy and don't forget to invite good company over to show off your new cooking prowess.

  • 4 chicken thigs
  • 4 tbs curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 medium red pepper
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • garlic
  • 1 cup rice
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1 cup chopped broccoli
  • 2 tbs maple syrup or brown sugar

Steps

  1. cube the chicken thigs and begin to satee in sauce pan for 5 minutes
  2. cube and add yellow onion, red pepper, and carrots
  3. after ten minutes open coconut can and poor over veggies and chicken
  4. add 4 tbs curry paste, and 2 tbs maple syrup or brown sugar
  5. simmer on medium low for 15 min
  6. serve over rice
