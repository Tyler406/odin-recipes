Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Quick Vegan Dish

Ever in the mood for a vegan dish that is easy to make? Well look no further. This quick recipe takes no time at all and the prep is easy, we hope you enjoy!

  • 4 medium yams
  • 1 can black beans (or kidney if you prefer)
  • 1 can sweet corn
  • green onion
  • vegan mayonaise
  • vegan shredded mozzorella cheese
  • 1 large tomato of choice (we like roma)

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. wash the yams and place on a baking sheet
  3. set the timer for 1 hour (baking time will vary)
  4. rinse and drain corn and beans
  5. cube tomatoes and chop green onion. Set aside for later
  6. stick a fork in the yams to check if they are done (they should be soft)
  7. cut the yams open and place everything in whatever order you like!
